story

You are a 9 year old. Your parent has logged into the program and select the appropriate age group. You are presented with the book selection screen.

1. pick a book

you are presented with the first page of the book. you want the book to read to you first.

2. select the read to me button and listen to the book.

you now want to read the book. 

3. select read to book button.

you think you have made some reading errors and want to review.

4. select correct my mistakes 

you have finished the book and want to go back to the book selection screen.

5. go back to the book selection screen

you find another book you want to read and select it.

6. repeat steps 2-4
